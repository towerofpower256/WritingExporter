Alice the Fat Cheerleader
by mollycoddles (52)
Rated: 18+ · Interactive Story · Adult · #1819934
An interactive version of Mollycoddles' epic weight gain saga about plump cheerleaders.
Intro Rated: E

Modified: May 30th, 2019 at 12:52pm
 
Location: My Portfolio
Genres: Adult, Erotica, Teen
Access: Registered Users and higher only.
This is an interactive story containing 751 chapters. Each chapter tells part of the story and often ends with multiple choices. Click a choice and move to the next chapter in your story. When you reach a chapter that hasn't been written yet, don't be shy... make an addition!
The creator of this Interactive Story provides this information and guidance:
People are always asking for specific things to happen in the stories I write. So I thought I'd make an interactive story version, so that anyone can enjoy their own versions.


There's no rules, although I'm reserving the right to continue the storyline 1-1-1-1-1 etc as the canonical Alice story. But have fun writing whatever you want on all other storylines. The only rule is that this is a weight gain story! So keep these girls gaining!

Here's the basic gist: Alice is a plump blonde cheerleader who can't resist food. She is on a cheer team with Laurie, a busty mean girl who similarly has a sweet tooth, and Jen, an auburn-haired ditz who gains mostly in her behind. Other than that, I guess you'll figure it out as the story goes along. It's not exactly brain surgery XD
